# Privacy Policy for UPSC Control Room

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2024

## Overview

UPSC Control Room is a productivity Chrome extension designed for UPSC Civil Services aspirants. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Focus time statistics** - Hours spent studying
- **Streak data** - Consecutive days of activity
- **XP and rank progress** - Gamification metrics
- **Blocked site attempts** - Count of distracting sites blocked
- **Bookmarks** - URLs you choose to save
- **Quick notes** - Text notes you create

### What We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Browsing history
- Passwords or financial data
- Location data
- Contact information

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.**

- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- Data remains entirely on your computer

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your progress, settings, and notes locally |
| `tabs` | Monitor and block distracting websites |
| `alarms` | Track focus time and maintain streaks |
| `notifications` | Alert you about achievements and blocked sites |
| `contextMenus` | Enable right-click bookmark feature |
| `downloads` | Save your progress card as an image |
| `<all_urls>` | Block distracting sites across all websites |

## Why We Need `<all_urls>` Permission

This permission is **essential** for the core functionality:
1. To detect when you visit a distracting website (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
2. To redirect you to the blocked page
3. To track focus time while browsing educational content

**We do NOT:**
- Read page content
- Collect browsing data
- Send any information externally

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Right-click the extension icon
2. Click "Remove from Chrome"
3. All locally stored data is automatically deleted
